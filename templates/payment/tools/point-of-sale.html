Pesaify Checkout

Pesaify Checkout is a fast, simple interface for retail and mobile businesses to accept Bitcoin payments.

To compliment an existing point of sale system, we recommend merchants enter orders in the existing system (either in a custom "bitcoin" payment method or a "house account"), and then collect the payment separately with Pesaify Checkout.

Visit https://www.pesaify.com/docs/retail for more information on integrating Pesaify Checkout into your business.

Install the App

To pair use your pesaify app:

  1. Open Open on your phone
  2. Point your phone to this screen to capture the code
